Your Top 10 Questions Answered!

Everything you need to know about Purrify's activated coconut carbon odor eliminator

1. What exactly is Purrify?
Purrify is a premium odor eliminator made from 100% activated coconut carbon. It's specifically designed for cat litter boxes and provides natural, chemical-free odor control for up to 7 days. Unlike air fresheners that mask smells, Purrify actually captures and neutralizes odor molecules at the source.
2. How does activated coconut carbon work?
Activated coconut carbon has millions of tiny pores that trap odor molecules like a super-absorbent sponge. Through a special heat treatment process, the carbon becomes ultra-porous with incredible absorption capacity. It's the same technology used in premium water filters and air purification systems!
3. How do I use Purrify?
It's incredibly simple! After cleaning your litter box and adding fresh litter, sprinkle 2-3 tablespoons of Purrify evenly across the top. Gently mix it into the litter. That's it! Reapply every 7 days or when you completely change the litter.
4. Is Purrify safe for my cat?
Absolutely! Purrify is 100% non-toxic and safe for cats of all ages, including kittens. It contains no chemicals, fragrances, or artificial ingredients. It's also safe for multi-cat households and won't irritate sensitive paws. Even if your curious cat investigates or tracks some, it's completely harmless.
5. What types of litter does it work with?
Purrify works with ALL types of cat litter - clay, clumping, crystal, natural, pine, paper, you name it! It's also compatible with all litter box types, including automatic and self-cleaning models. The carbon granules blend seamlessly without affecting litter texture or clumping properties.
6. Will my cat track Purrify through the house?
No! Purrify is formulated to stay in the litter box. The activated carbon granules are designed to mix with litter, not stick to paws. Once mixed in, it stays where it belongs.
7. How long does one bottle last?
Each application lasts 7 days. A standard 12 oz bottle provides approximately 6 applications, giving you about 6 weeks of odor control. For multi-cat households, you may want to reapply every 5-6 days for optimal results.
8. Is Purrify environmentally friendly?
Yes! Purrify is made from renewable coconut sources and is fully biodegradable. The packaging is recyclable, and there are zero harmful environmental impacts. By extending the life of your litter, it also helps reduce waste!
9. Can I use it with baking soda or other deodorizers?
You can, but most customers find they don't need additional products once they start using Purrify. It's actually more effective on its own. However, it won't interfere with other products if you choose to use them together.
10. What if I'm not satisfied with the results?
We stand behind Purrify with a satisfaction guarantee! If you're not completely happy with the results, contact the store where you purchased it or reach out to us directly. We're confident you'll love how it keeps your home fresh and odor-free.
