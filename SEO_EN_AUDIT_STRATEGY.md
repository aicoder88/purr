**Date:** February 7, 2026  
**Language:** English (en-CA, en-US)  
**Total Pages:** 15  
**Document Type:** Strategic Audit for Implementation

---

## Executive Summary

This audit covers **15 core English pages** across three tiers of strategic importance. Current SEO implementation shows strong foundation with opportunities for significant improvement in title optimization, description refinement, and keyword targeting alignment with search intent.

### Key Findings
- ✅ Strong brand positioning with "water-filter grade activated carbon" USP
- ✅ Good use of social proof (87% upgrade rate, 10,000+ customers)
- ⚠️ Several titles exceed 60 characters
- ⚠️ Some descriptions exceed 155 characters
- ⚠️ Missing high-value keywords in several pages
- ⚠️ CTA language could be more action-oriented
