## ‚úèÔ∏è Rewrite Strategy

### For Conflicting Pages:
1. **Make them shorter and more direct**
2. **Use different format:** checklist, comparison table, FAQ style
3. **Focus on immediate actionable answer** vs comprehensive education
4. **Add unique angles:** diagnostic tool, decision framework, quick reference

### URL Structure Note:
- Blog posts: `/blog/[slug]` (comprehensive)
- Learn pages: `/learn/[topic]` (educational)
- PAA pages: `/learn/answers/[slug]` (quick answers)

This structure signals to Google:
- Blog = Deep content (read when researching)
- Learn = Educational resource (understand the topic)
- Answers = Quick answers (get immediate solution)
