# Product Overview

Purrify is an e-commerce platform selling activated carbon cat litter deodorizer that eliminates odors naturally.

## Business Model

- **Product Line**: Activated carbon cat litter additive in three sizes (20g trial, 60g standard, 140g family pack)
- **Revenue**: Direct-to-consumer via Stripe checkout, B2B retailer partnerships
- **Markets**: Multi-language support (English, French, Chinese) targeting Canada, US, and diaspora communities
- **Distribution**: Online sales with optimized checkout flow, retail partner network

## Key Features

- SEO-optimized blog system for content marketing
- Educational content (science, safety, how-it-works)
- Location-based landing pages for Canadian cities/provinces
- Customer referral program
- B2B retailer portal and inquiry system
- Admin dashboard for blog management and analytics

## Value Proposition

Natural, pet-safe alternative to chemical deodorizers using science-backed activated carbon technology. Cost-effective for multi-cat households with proven odor elimination.

## Customer Journey

Discovery (SEO/blog) → Education (learn section) → Social proof (testimonials) → Purchase (Stripe checkout) → Retention (referrals/email)
