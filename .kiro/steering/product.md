# Product Overview

Purrify is an e-commerce platform selling activated carbon cat litter additive that eliminates odors at the source.

## Core Business Model

- **Primary Product**: Activated carbon cat litter deodorizer in three sizes (trial, standard, family pack)
- **Revenue Streams**: Direct-to-consumer sales via Stripe, B2B retailer partnerships
- **Target Markets**: English (Canada/US), French (Quebec), Chinese (diaspora)
- **Distribution**: Online sales with Stripe checkout, retail partner network

## Key Features

- Multi-language e-commerce (EN/FR/ZH)
- Content marketing via SEO-optimized blog
- Educational content (how-it-works, safety, science)
- Location-based landing pages for Canadian cities
- Referral program for customer acquisition
- B2B retailer portal and inquiry system

## Product Positioning

- Natural, safe alternative to chemical deodorizers
- Science-backed activated carbon technology
- Pet-safe and environmentally friendly
- Cost-effective solution for multi-cat households

## Customer Journey

1. Discovery via SEO content (blog posts, solution pages)
2. Education through learn section (science, safety, usage)
3. Social proof via testimonials and case studies
4. Purchase through optimized checkout flow
5. Retention via referral program and email marketing
